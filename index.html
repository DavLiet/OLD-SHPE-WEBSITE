We Are SHPE

Our mission is to change lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support and development. Washington University in Saint Louis is home to a vibrant muti-ethnic community of Scholars, Leaders, and Innovators . As Scholars, we are constantly asking questions. The most important of which has been: How may we help? As Leaders, we are aware of our underrepresentation in the STEM workforce. We are working to change that. As Innovators, we are taking every step possible to creatively engage with our community.
